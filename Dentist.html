Frequently Answered Questions

Got questions about pricing, treatments, or insurance? We have gathered answers to help guide your visit.

Do you accept local health insurance?

Yes, Apex Dental works with major PPO insurance providers. Our reception team will run a complimentary benefits check for you upon request and files claims directly to minimize out-of-pocket costs.

How long does an Invisalign treatment take?

Standard Invisalign procedures average between 6 to 18 months, depending on alignment difficulty. You'll receive a set of digital aligner trays that are updated every 1 to 2 weeks.

What is your emergency walk-in policy?

If you are suffering from acute toothaches, swelling, or structural fractures, you can walk straight into our clinic. We save active scheduling slots specifically to handle same-day emergency treatments.

Are clinical tooth whitening procedures safe?

Absolutely. Our in-office whitening procedures use regulated hydrogen peroxide gels and protective isolation shields, meaning there is zero risk to enamel health and minimal tooth sensitivity.
